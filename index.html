Research

Research-focus:traits-based macroecology and macroevolution

My research mainly focused on two topics: (1) the hydraulic traits of angiosperms and their significance in ecophysiology, macroecology, and macroevolution; and (2) the climatic niches (both physiological and ecological) of tropical and subtropical plants.

My work integrates field observations, experimental studies, and computational modeling to understand plant adaptations to environmental changes and their evolutionary implications.
